About SpaceX

SPACEX was established in 2010 in the US. This company is owned by an anonymous person. The company's categories mainly focus on developing space tourism packages, including trips to low Earth orbit or the International Space Station for wealthy individuals or groups, and partnering with government space agencies to offer trips to the Moon or Mars for research or scientific purposes.

SpaceX is also researching and making reusable space vehicles to make space travel cheaper and easier for more people. According to the CEO of this company in the UK " SpaceX is developing space-based solar power stations to provide a sustainable energy source for Earth and offering space-based manufacturing and assembly services to companies looking to take advantage of the unique properties of microgravity environments."
